About GWPC Bhopal

Government Women’s Polytechnic College, Bhopal was established in the year 1964 under the Technical Education, Skill Development & Employment Deptt., Govt. of M.P. The college is situated at the center of the city. Affiliated to the Rajiv Gandhi Technical University, it was the first College of the state which has been established exclusively for women for imparting technical education ...
